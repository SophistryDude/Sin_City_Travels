**Survey Date**: February 3, 2026

## Key Findings

### Google Indoor Maps Status

**Historical Context**:
- Google [launched indoor maps for Las Vegas casinos in 2012](https://maps.googleblog.com/2012/01/get-indoor-maps-of-las-vegas-casinos.html)
- Initially released at Consumer Electronics Show (CES) 2012

**Current Status (2026)**:
- ‚ùå Google Indoor Maps coverage for Las Vegas casinos is **limited or discontinued**
- Most major casinos do NOT appear in Google Indoor Maps
- Travelers rely on third-party PDF maps and casino websites
- Possible reasons: Wi-Fi connectivity issues, data privacy, or Google discontinuing the service

**Verdict**: Google Indoor Maps is NOT a reliable data source for Sin City Travels.

## Legal Considerations

**Copyright**:
- Casino floor plans are copyrighted by the property owners
- Request permission before using in commercial app
- Attribution required for publicly available maps

**Fair Use**:
- Educational/research use may be covered
- Consult legal advisor before launch

**Photography Policies**:
- Many casinos prohibit photos on casino floor
- Hotel lobbies and public areas usually allowed
- Check individual property policies
